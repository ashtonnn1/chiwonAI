The Problem

01

Every AI built for enterprise sales is trained on seller data. So it optimizes for seller outcomes. The buyer never had a voice in the model.

02

The major platforms give access to whoever pays most. OpenAI. Google. Meta. Recommendations aren't neutral — they're auctioned. That's the infrastructure commerce is being built on.

03

Good companies get punished for doing the right thing. Competing against vendors who manipulate. Rebuilding trust with buyers already burned. The system rewards extraction — even honest sellers get pulled toward it.

04

The norms are being set right now. AI agents will run this playbook across every commercial transaction at machine speed. Nobody is making sure the defaults are right.

Our Approach

Bilateral intelligence — trained on both buyer and seller signal simultaneously.
